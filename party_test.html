• SARAH VANLANDINGHAM

    Sarah is originally from State College, Pennsylvania. She met Liz in graduate school at Penn where they took breaks from learning about preserving old buildings to sample Philadelphia's best happy hours. They became close friends and have taken plenty of opportunities since graduating for "girls weekends" to get together and stay updated on each other's lives. Sarah works for the District government in Washington, DC as a Historic Preservation Specialist. She looks forward to many visits to Philadelphia in preparation for the upcoming wedding!

  • SABRINA CUSIMANO
    Matron of Honor

    Though born in New Orleans, Sabrina has been marooned above the Mason Dixon line since graduating from LSU. She works for an NGO affiliated with the United Nations Department of Public Information and UNESCO (UN Economic and Social Council). In her spare time, she collects and sells china. She is the creator of the website – MoshiMotif.com – which references china patterns on TV shows and movies. Being Elizabeth’s older sister, Sabrina has known Liz for almost her entire life (Sabrina was sadly stuck at home with the babysitter when Elizabeth was born at the hospital). Sabrina and Elizabeth have been through many great times (and some not so great times) together...  reading books as kids, fantastic birthday parties, costumes, braiding hair, endless dance recitals, Lille, many moves, job searches, Las Vegas, heartbreaks, museums, concerts, Bruges, Bermuda, personal tragedies, national tragedies, Paris, restaurants, When Parents Text, nights out, nights in, and pretty much everything in between.

  • JILL TAYLOR

    The shenanigans started for Liz and Jill circa 2003, back when they were still riding the bus to high school in Chalmette, Lousiana. Things only escalated from there. From nerding out in the Chemistry Club to feigning sophistication at an art gallery, they never had a dull moment while rooming together during two years of undergrad at LSU. Some of Jill's fondest memories with Liz are from their shared travels, including Chicago and Washington D.C., with Gatlinburg as her favorite escapade. Jill is ecstatic about her next excursion from Baton Rouge to Philly to witness the moment when Liz and http://futuremrandmrs.covolus.com/wp-admin/media-upload.php?post_id=621&type=image&TB_iframe=1Nick's Happily Ever After begins.

  • CAITLIN WATKINS

    Caitlin is eighteen years old and finishing up her senior year of high school. She lives in Merrimack, New Hampshire with her family. She will be majoring in elementary education in college next year. Caitlin is Liz's goddaughter and has forever been a big part of her life. They both enjoy creative art and have recently painted pottery together. Caitlin and Liz also share a love for Sephora face masks. Caitlin cannot wait to be a part of Liz and Nick's wedding!

  • MELISSA CALLOWAY

    From DeRidder, Louisiana, Melissa now lives in Austin, Texas and is a writer and also a counselor at the Art Institute. Melissa is a new aunt and loves spending time with her family and her dog named Bear. Liz met Melissa through mutual friends at Louisiana State University and quickly became friends and became roommates as well. Melissa and Liz share odd senses of humor and a love for wine and people watching. Melissa looks forward to visiting Liz in Philly again soon!

  • KASEY DISERENS

    Originally from New York, Kasey and Liz met when they both moved to Philadelphia for graduate school in Historic Preservation. They were friends from the very beginning, Kasey happily pairing up with Liz as her lab partner for a chemistry based materials science class (Kasey learned so much about chemicals from Liz). After graduation, Liz stayed on in Philly to work, Kasey stayed on for...more school. Currently in a PhD program, when time allows Kasey enjoys meeting up with Liz (and Nick) to test out new beers and restaurants in Philly. Liz and Kasey have also taken some exciting trips together! Perhaps the most memorable involved camping in a ghost town in the middle of Montana, and having the tent collapse on them in the middle of the night so that they were then forced to sleep in a tiny two-door car. She looks forward to many new adventures with the happy couple!
