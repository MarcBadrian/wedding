Bridesmaids

  • PAMELA BUCHWALTER
    Maid of Honor



    Pamela may be my little sister, but I look up to her. She's the strongest person I know, in more ways than one. Pamela has been with me through every moment of my life. She was even able to keep Marc's proposal a secret and fly out for the whole thing! You can't pick your sister, but you can pick your best friend. Pamela is my best friend. We've been through more ups and downs together than with anyone else. We'd do anything for each other.

  • ASHLEY BROWN


    Ashley and I met thanks to the Greek system. She's my “little little!” We love hard, work hard, party hard, and try to live life to the fullest. Despite what may be going on in Ashley's life, her carefree attitude is contagious! Ashley is always the person that I go to when it comes to getting advice on things like fashion, planning something of any kind (cough, cough, including the wedding), or simply getting an opinion on a problem I'm having. We love talking about fitness and sharing new workouts that we're trying! Ashley has one up on me in the kitchen though. I envy her amazing cooking skills and the pictures she always sends me of her creative, healthy and somehow delicious looking lunches and dinners!

  • RACHEL BUCHWALTER


    My cousin Rachel has been in my life since I was born. She's not only a part of some of my earliest childhood memories, but also the amazing memory of the day of my engagement. Rachel was there with Pamela watching it all happen! We continued to get closer as we got older and realized that we have so much in common. Not only are we family, but we're friends. I don't know what I'd do without Rachel's sound advice and her superpower of being able to make me feel better when I call her to vent about having a bad day.

  • JON "JAG" GAY


    I met Jag back in 2009 at a banquet for Z89, our college radio station. I was still a student looking to network with as many people as possible. Jag was one of the first people to really take the time to listen to my stuff and give me advice. I listened to his aircheck too and we instantly bonded over our love of radio! I never thought that what started out as a networking relationship would turn into me finding a forever friend. We always joke that I'm the female version of him and he's the male version of me. Jag even came up with the term “man of honor” since he hated when I would call him a “bridesdude.”

  • AMANDA JOSEPH


    Amanda and I became sorority sisters and “twins” in college back in 2009, but I consider us twins and sisters in real life as well. We immediately bonded over our love of The Beatles and the fact that we will always be ourselves, no matter what anyone thinks. I instantly fell in love with her spunky personality, her ability to make people smile, and the fact that she just tells it how it is. There's not a thing Amanda doesn't know about me. Oh, and I'm jealous of her awesome painting skills!

  • JIMMY SIMMONS


    I've known Jimmy for over ten years! We met our freshman year of college. He was my next-door neighbor. From partying, breakups and late night conversations to lifting each other off the floor and literally carrying each other home, Jimmy has seen me at my best and has helped me at my worst! A true Bostonian, Jimbo's humor and awesome accent instantly made me want to be his friend. He's the funniest guy I know. If you really want to laugh, ask him to say the word “apartment.” Jimmy is stored in my cellphone as “Jimmy is amazin” for a reason… mainly because he did it himself.

  • LAURA WILONS


    Laura was one of the very first people I met when I moved to Memphis four years ago. Not knowing many people, I spent a lot of time at the gym. One day, I was working out on the elliptical next to Laura and she was bold enough to introduce herself! I knew in that moment that we'd be friends immediately. Now, I don't like walking into a class at the gym without her. We have such a similar backstory when it comes to life experiences and how we were raised. We just get each other. Laura is the most selfless person I know. Even though she still hasn't gotten me to try tofu pepperoni, she'll tell you that there's still time.

 

 

Groomsmen

  • NEAL BARENBLAT
    Best Man

He's not only one of my best friends, he's my Best Man.

  • CASE CRITCHLOW

He loved movies, I loved movies – the rest was history. Whether you want to learn about the history of Memphis BBQ, take in a few obscure cult horror films, go fishing, or celebrate Cinco De Mayo, Case is the man with the plan.
