Our Story

Michelle was born and raised in Rockaway Beach, NY and Marc was born and raised in Memphis, TN. She went to Syracuse University for college and he went to the University of Miami. The odds that they would ever meet were slim. Thankfully, Michelle's career in radio found her taking a gig in Memphis. Since she was moving to a brand new city and didn't know anyone, her friend and now bridesmaid suggested she try online dating. Michelle signed up for OkCupid, where she and Marc had a 94% match rate. After a few message exchanges back and forth, the two of them met up at Republic Coffee, a local coffee shop. Two skinny honey vanilla cream lattes and FIVE HOURS LATER, the rest is history. They both knew after that first encounter that this was the start of something very special.
